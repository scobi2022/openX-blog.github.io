October 9, 2024

As part of the Infra Wiss Blogs (Pampel and Rothfritz 2024) project, we are organizing a first networking workshop for blogging researchers and employees from infrastructure facilities on 06.12.2024.

The aim is to share initial experiences of the various stakeholders and promote their exchange. We will also present the initial results of our survey of German science blogs.

When? Wednesday, 06.12.2024, 10:30-15:00

Where? Robert-Koch-Forum of the Einstein Center Digital Future (ECDF), Wilhelmstraße 67, 10117 Berlin

We are currently planning with the following program:

Time Topic
10:30 Welcome
11:00 Presentation of the results of the mapping of the German science blog landscape.
11:30 Coffee Break
12:00 Presentation by Henning Krause (Helmholtz Blogs) (angefragt)
12:30 Presentation by Martin Fenner (Front Matter)
13:00 Food break
13:30 Impulses from practice
14:00 Discussion round 1
14:30 Discussion round 2
15:00 Summary of the results
15:15 End of the Workshop

The workshop will be organized in German. Registration is possible through the website of our research group.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact Catharina Ochsner directly, who is managing the project for us. Her email address is: catharina.ochsner@hu-berlin.de

Further information about the project can be found on the new website of the Infra Wiss Blogs site at: https://infrawissblogs.org/. In the future, we will also report on the project’s progress in German on this website, where we will organize a project blog.

Additional workshops are planned for the coming year. 

The “First come, first served” motto applies. We have space for about 20 people. We look forward to your participation. Registrations are possible until the 20th of October 2024.

Further information about the research group can be found on our official website.
